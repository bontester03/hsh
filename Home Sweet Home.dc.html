About HSH

Your home away from home

Our Philosophy

We provide a "home away from home" that reinforces the importance of education, love, and a supportive, creative environment. Every child is gifted in their own unique way. We view children as capable, competent co-contributors and active participants in their own learning — blending child-initiated learning, adult-enriched learning, children's interests, and intentional teaching.

Our Vision

Inspiring children to encounter the world with joy, curiosity and confidence — feeling loved, respected and encouraged to reach their fullest potential.

Our Mission

"Our home where we Create… Play… Explore!" A program designed for the well-rounded development of children — meeting and exceeding expectations of care and learning.
